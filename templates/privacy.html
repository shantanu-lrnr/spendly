Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps, used to detect suspicious activity.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the Service:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within your account.
  • To authenticate you securely when you sign in.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., password reset), if applicable.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days. We do not sell, rent, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social login providers. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an update to this policy before they are enabled.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
